Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2025

Focus25 ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data when you use our app.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users of the Focus25 app.

2. Local Data Storage Only

All data created or entered by you while using the app—such as timer sessions, preferences, and focus logs—is stored only on your device. This data is not transmitted to any external servers or third-party services.

3. No Third-Party Integrations

Focus25 does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or external data processing tools.

4. Children's Privacy

Focus25 is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or retain any data from children.

5. Your Control

  • You control all data within the app.
  • You may delete the app at any time to remove all locally stored data.
  • We do not have access to recover or retrieve your data.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the app. Updates will be available on this page, and significant changes may be announced in-app.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, you can reach us at:

  • Email: privacy@focus25app.com
  • Support: Help Center

This Privacy Policy is effective as of July 4, 2025, and applies to all users of the Focus25 app.
